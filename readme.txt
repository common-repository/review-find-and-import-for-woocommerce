= Introduction =

The Review Find and Import for Woocommerce plugin searches the web for reviews and ratings of the products in your store. You can import these reviews, use them for oposition research, optimize your catelog, make selling decissions and much more.  With this <a title="Product Review Plugin for Woocommerce" href="https://www.apigenius.io/software/find-import-reviews-woocommerce/" target="_blank">Woocommerce Review Plugin</a> you can automate the process of finding and importing product reviews from across the web. Update the reviews for 1,000's of products in minutes.  These are all real reviews left by real buyers.

Product reviews provide a huge boost in virtually every imaginable eCommerce Key Performance Indicator like average time on page, conversion rates, average cart value, customer engagement and countless others. They will also give you an advantage over competitors in organic search.

<strong>Increased Revenue</strong> - Products with review always have higher conversion rates, which means more sales and increased revenue.

<strong>Real Reviews</strong> - These are all real reviews for the products in your store. They will be actual ratings on features, functionality, price and more.

<strong>Review Search</strong> - This Woocommerce Plugin automates the process of searching for product reviews. With one click it scours multiple data sources for 1, 10, 50, 200 products at a time.

<strong>Rating Filter</strong> - Set a simple option to only import reviews above a specific star rating. Want to only import 4 stars and up, no problem.

<strong>Make Selling Decisions</strong> - Import reviews to see what people are saying about the products in your store. You may want to stop selling products getting bad reviews or prioritize those that are getting settler ratings.

<strong>Better Rankings</strong> - It is a simple fact that products with reviews will get better rankings in the search results. This plugin automates that process.

<strong>Higher Click Through Rates</strong> - Products with stars in the organic search results on Google & Bing are clicked on more often increasing traffic and subsequent ranking position.

<strong>Social Shares</strong> - Use a 3rd party service to share reviews on social media platforms as they are being imported into your store. Products with reviews are also shared more by users.

== Installation ==

1. Upload the plugin folder to the /wp-content/plugins/ directory.
2. Activate the plugin through the Plugins menu in WordPress.
3. Get an API key from ApiGenius.io
4. Configure the plugin options.
